4MAT FRAMEWORK
--------------
Every story follows this rhythm:
1. WHY – Purpose or tension
   Example: "Racing is changing—but ownership hasn't caught up."

2. WHAT – Proof, context, or fact
   Example: "Through digital-syndication, we're opening ownership to anyone, globally."

3. HOW – The mechanism or model
   Example: "Fractional leases, fixed terms, full compliance—all managed through a regulated platform."

4. WHAT IF – Vision or invitation
   Example: "From paddocks to protocols—this is ownership, evolved."
